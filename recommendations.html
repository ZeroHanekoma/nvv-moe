stuff i recommend

taste is subjective, so i can objectively say my taste is superior. some of this is mainstream stuff that i actually enjoyed, which is surprisingly rare.

video games

  • Team Fortress 2 - hat trading simulator, with a fun first person shooter minigame in it!
  • Noita - insanely difficult roguelite about gold and unintentional suicide. lots of fire.
  • Metal Gear Solid 3 - the best war story ever told.
  • Neotokyo - one of the best free, dead online shooters. beautiful map design. very intense. no one plays anymore...
  • Dragon's Dogma - japanese take on a western rpg. came out at the same time as Skyrim and Dark Souls. slow burning start, but once it picks up THE WIND WILL BE PUSHING YOU.
  • Devil May Cry 1, 3, 4, and 5 - stylish action games
  • Viewtiful Joe 1&2 - stylish and viewtiful side-scrolling beat 'em up
  • Dragon's Crown - good fantasy side scrolling beat 'em up. a lot like Shadow Over Mystara
  • Starsector - space game. smuggle drugs, hunt pirates, build a colony, or just go on a genocidal rampage.
  • Fate/Stay Night - look... i just think the whole concept is really cool and the world-building is really well done. the sex scenes are really bad, but fun for a laugh.

anime & manga

  • FLCL - good show.
  • Nichijou - slice of life about high school girls. has no right being as funny as it is.
  • Berserk - if you've never read it, everything everyone says is 100% true. it is actually that good.
  • Welcome to the NHK - it's a comedy. i'm not crying, it's just the rain.
  • KonoSuba - i gave this a pass initially because it's isekai kusoge. it's actually really, really funny.
  • TRASH - both one of the most edgy and possibly CONTROVERSIAL mango i've read. also one of the most violent and badass. not for the faint of heart, or anyone with self respect.
  • Ghost in the Shell - the movie is great, Stand Alone Complex is fun, i haven't seen much of the rest.
  • Dorohedoro - never saw the animu. the manga is art porn, and the story is an absolute joy ride of violence and mindfuckery
  • 20th Century Boys - a work of goddamn art. this should be more popular.
  • Mahou Shoujo Madoka Magica - a fun, cute show about magical girls where nothing bad happens to anyone. don't you want to be a magical girl?
  • Frieren - made me cry like a little girl. multiple times.
  • Kara no Kyoukai - shiki is such a fucking badass. i also like nasu's pseud-philosophy.

movies & shows

  • Aliens - the first two movies are pretty much my favorite movies ever.
  • Paprika - i don't care that it's animu. it's one of the best movies ever.
  • Shoot 'em Up - a homeless dude who likes carrots finds a baby. proceeds to kill everyone he meets.
  • Happy! - a schizophrenic loses his child. proceeds to kill everyone he meets.
  • X-Files - totally not a documentary.
  • Hot Fuzz - just a great movie all around. the greater good.
  • The Men Who Stare at Goats - weird flick about a very real US Government Project. seriously.
  • Little Miss Sunshine - ugly duckling child wants to be a beauty pageant star. her family is incredibly disfunctional. hijinx ensue.
  • Sucker Punch - fuck you, i liked it.
  • Cabin in the Woods - "horror movie". everyone dies.
  • The Color From Out of Space - Nic Cage stars in a HP Lovecraft story. Need I say more?
  • Police Squad - the funniest shit that has ever aired on TV. murdered after the first season, unironically for being too funny for TV at the time.
  • Whitest Kids 'U Know - rip Trevor Moore. The Popcorn Factory Sketch is my favorite, followed by Little Hitler
  • Dead Poet's Society - Oh Captain, My Captain.

books

  • VALIS - philip k dick documents his mental breakdown.
  • Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep? - Blade Runner. The book is actually better. Movie is still a masterpiece.
  • Schrodinger's Cat Trilogy - 1960s book about... I don't actually know. Raunchy, trippy, and lots of fun.
  • Illuminatis Trilogy - 1960s book by two Playboy Editors. What if every single conspiracy theory was true?
  • Angels and Demons - prequel to the DaVinci Code. these books are insanely good, never saw the movie.
  • Holes - Young Adult book I still appreciate in my old age.
  • The Phantom Toolbooth - children's book about language and perception. really cute and fun.
  • Transmetropolitan - one of two comics. journalist in the future tries to report the truth. no one is ready for it.
  • The Invisibles - the other comic book. absolute mindfuck.
  • Behold a Pale Horse - grandpappy of most modern day conspiracy theories. incredibly difficult to parse, but it's worth it for the unhinged schizo rants. how much is true and how much is bs? you decide.
